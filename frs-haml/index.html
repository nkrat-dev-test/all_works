What is the FSS?

Fiscal settlement system is an automated system for accounting the cash transactions, which is implemented as a cloud solution and available to participants via a secure Internet communication channels

Sellers of goods and services, after the connection to the FSS, can use smartphones, tablets, computers, self-service terminals as registrars of settlement transactions in order to perform fiscal functions.

Legislation

The Law of Ukraine "On amendments to the Tax code of Ukraine and certain legislative acts of Ukraine regarding tax reform" N71-VIII was adopted on 28 December 2014

Since 1 July 2015 the tax reform provides a gradual extension of obligation to the use the RST (registrar of settlement transactions) for all financial institutions, National postal operator and other legal entities

Legislative requirements for the timing of implementing the registration of settlement transactions

1 Entrepreneurs - unified tax payers of the 3 group (income of 1.5 million to 20 million UAH per year) since 1 July 2015
2 Entrepreneurs - unified tax payers of the 2 group (income from 300 thousand to 1.5 million UAH per year), when the income exceeding 1 million UAH per year since 1 January 2016
3 Operators of self-service terminals and accounts depositing, regardless of the form of ownership since 1 January 2016
4 Non-bank financial institutions since 1 January 2016
