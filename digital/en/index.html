ABOUT

Netopay Digital – is a service that allows consumer to make payment for digital goods and services through the self-service terminal (Cash-in), retailer’s cash department and point of sale, currency exchange office and the Internet

Digital goods and services are products supplied in the digital form, such as game-money, software, video and audio content, etc.

How it works

IMPORTANT

In the Netopay Digital terms the digital goods mean a special code in electronic form – the Quant or Quantum
Each Quant represents a unique set of data which makes its particular parts work in a result of interaction between the goods or services of the producer
The legal definition of the Quant is a legal significant requirement to producers for receiving goods or services in scale set in advance
In terms of regulatory and supervisory authority of Ukraine, Quant is the access code to the program complexes and systems of producers, Import and distribution of the plug on the territory of Ukraine is authorized and regulated by law, legal acts and regulations
From a technical standpoint, Quant is an encrypted document in electronic form, which contains all information about the producer, the product (or service), its cost, delivery and use, as well as other necessary information for carrying out a transaction
From a financial standpoint, Quant is a unique accounting tool box in an electronic form, and the transaction with its use is instantaneous and irrevocable. All transactions are carried out through the Netopay Digital service

LEGISLATION

  • The activities of the service are fully carried out in accordance with the local and international legislative norms and rules of the financial markets in various geographical regions
  • The legislative norms and requirements on counteraction to financing of terrorism and money laundering (FATF) are considered in agreement between all the participants of the service
  • All participants of the system are a subject to be checked according to KYC specifications with objective of counteraction to money laundering
  • All agreements comply with OLAF requirements
  • All users of the service sign an agreement/confirm an agreement with “Anti-Money Laundering Agreement“ and „Anti-Financial Fraud Compliance“
