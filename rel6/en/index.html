About project

Netopay Finance is a regional financial ecosystem that enables the creation of innovative and successful region

Netopay Finance provides the use of universal payment arrangements in electronic form, the implementation of which contributes to the effective and transparent interaction between the public, business and regional (municipal) authorities.

Advantages

  • Increase of welfare and living standards of population
  • Establishing the equal conditions for doing business and reducing the tax burden
  • Business development and creating new jobs
  • Targeted and transparent use of public funds
  • Improvement of infrastructure, level of social security and welfare of the region
  • Increase of social attractiveness of the region
  • Детінізація економіки та захист від корупції

How it works

Netopay Finance implementation provides the organization of mutual settlements within the region between residents, businesses and authorities using the equivalent of values in electronic form

Quant.Regio is an equivalent of values, which is used as payment unit for settlements in Netopay Finance.

Region's rules

The principle of differentiated allocation of contributions to the budget of the region is implemented in Netopay Finance, it eliminates the corruption and the misuse of paid taxes and fees. Each region establishes its own guidelines for the allocation of contributions and sets conditions of refunds to taxpayers if they follow the rules of the region. For example, if a person does not smoke and maintains a healthy lifestyle, he/she will be automatically refunded with the amount of paid deductions, which were targeted for the development of medicine in the region.
